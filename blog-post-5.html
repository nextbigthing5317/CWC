Top 10 serials on Sun TV

  • 3 March 2022

10) Chandralekha:




Chandralekha is a Tamil serial on Sun TV that takes the tenth position with the TRP of 4.47. Meena and Vasundhara are sisters who give birth to their child at the same hospital at the same time. Lekha was born to Meena and Chandra was born to Vasundhara. Due to some kidnappers in the hospitals, the children were swapped. So Chandra was brought up by Meena and Lekha was brought up by Vasundhara.



9) Aruvi:




Aruvi is a Tamil serial on Sun TV that takes the ninth position with the TRP of 4.69. A girl named “Aruvi” is the heroine of this serial. She lost her mother when she was born. She was brought up by her father. She treats everyone in a normal way and does what she likes. A guy named “Pugazh” is the hero of this serial who was born in a rich family. His mother is very strict and a typical mother-in-law. Aruvi and Puzhal’s mother at their first meeting, have a fight which created bitterness between them. Later, Aruvi and Puzhal fell in love with each other. Aruvi was unaware that his mother is the one whom she fought. They both married each other and Aruvi was facing issues and lacked the right to speak in front of her mother-in-law.



8) Anbe Vaa:




Anbe Vaa is a Tamil serial on Sun TV that scores the eighth position in this list with the TRP of 5.87. A girl named “Bhoomika” is the heroine of this serial and the hero is named “Varun”. Bhoomika loves her mother and her sister. Varun is born in a rich family who is careless and less attentive to his work. Varun’s family members except his father will like Varun even though he is careless. Bhoomika and Varun fell in love with each other and get married.



7) Abiyum Naanum:




Abiyum Naanum is a Tamil serial on Sun TV that scores the eighth position in this list with the TRP of 5.94. Abi is an eight-year-old girl who was adopted by Saravan. Saravan will join as a driver in one of the rich families. Mukhil is also a small boy who is the son of Shiva and Meena. He is a pampered son. When Saravanan joins as a driver to this family, Abi also lives there. So Meena started liking Abi and showing love to her. Mukhil is jealous of the love that his mom gives to Abi. The twist in this serial is that Abi is the girl born to Shiva and Meena who lost Abi at a yearly stage. This is due to the astrological reason that when Abi is brought up by her parents, their parents will die when Abi is 9 years old.



6) Ethirneechal:




Ethirneechal is a serial that took sixth place in the list with the TRP of 6.96. Janani is a girl who is ambitious and finds a way to reach her goal. But her dream is shattered when she gets married to a patriarchal family. Whether Janani will proceed with her dream or she will live like a normal woman is the main focus of this serial.



5) Kannana Kanne:




Kannana Kanne is a serial that took fifth place in the list with the TRP of 8.68. This story is based on the longing bond between the daughter and her father. The father's hatred towards her daughter is because when she was born her mother died due to some health complications. So, the father started thinking that his wife died because of his daughter and started hatred. The daughter's name is ‘Meera’ who is the heroine of this serial. When Meera was young, his father married another lady to take care of Meera. Meera was brought up by her stepmother and stepsister. Now she became a grown-up girl even then her father doesn’t like her. Her stepmother and stepsister are trying to unite Meera and her father.



4) Vanathai Pola:




Vanathai Pola is a Tamil Serial that holds fourth place in the list with the TRP of 9.23. Vanathai Pola's serial is based on the bond between brother and sister who lost their parents when they are young. The sister is named “Thulasi” and the brother is named “Chinrasu”. Thulasi was brought up with her brother. When she attains the age of marriage, her brother started searching groom for her. Thulasi rejected every groom as she is not ready to leave her brother. But after some days, she likes a guy who is the panchayat board officer in that village and also nemesis for her brother.



3) Roja:




Roja serial is the third top serial with a TRP of 9.41. This serial is enacted by Priyanka Nalkari as ‘Roja’ and Sibbu Suryan ad ‘Arjun’. Roja is an orphan girl who was brought up in an orphanage by Shanthamoorthy. Arjun is a leading lawyer who handles criminal cases. Roja was in search of a lawyer to handle the case which was falsely put on Shanthamoorthy. Meanwhile, Arjun's family decides for his marriage with another girl who is not really good at her behavior. So Arjun doesn’t wish to marry her. One day, Roja found Arjun and seeks Arjun’s help to take up the cause. In return, Arjun asked for a one-year marriage contract. Roja accepted that and they both married and pretended as husband and wife. Gradually, Arjun fall in love with Roja for her humble and selfless nature and Roja also fall in love and they started living as a husband and wife.



2) Sundari:




Sundari is a Tamil serial on Sun TV that tops second in the list with a TRP of 9.63. This serial is mainly heroine-centric. Sundari is a character in this serial which is enacted by Gabriella Sellus. Sundari, a girl who is dark-skinned by birth, faces much criticism and discrimination in this society. Sundari’s aim is to be an IAS officer but she couldn’t pursue her studies since she is in a village. Karthick(Jishnu Menon) is cast as a hero in this serial. He married Sundari even though he didn’t like her because of her skin tone and he also underestimates Sundari. This serial would be more relatable for many of the Tamil people who face this kind of issue in this society. This serial also gives hope and positivity to many of the people who faced this problem. That could be the reason for this serial to top in this list.



1) Kayal:




Kayal is a Tamil serial which is telecasting on Sun Tv. This serial tops the list with a TRP of 10.57. The hero of this serial is Sanjeev Karthick who played a leading role as a serial actor in Vijay Television. The heroine of this serial is Chaitra Reddy who also played a leading role in Zee Tamil. In this serial, Chaitra Reddy is playing the role of Kayal who is an elder sister in her family. She is the only support for her whole family. Ezhilarasan (Karthick) who understands Kayal's struggles and hurdles will support her in every situation and problem which Kayal faces. The struggle of a selfless girl who wishes to support her family at any cause would be the reason for this serial to top the list.
