Unknown Facts about Cooku With Comali

  • 10 March 2022

25 Cooks were shortlisted:



Yes, we might think that Cooku with Comali team randomly selected the celebrities who are available for their show,but there is a lot of work being done just to select the contestants. The whole team was strict about two things, one is the contestant should know basic cooking and the next is they should be serious about the competition then only the combination of cook and comali would work well. Many contestants became passionate in cooking after joining cooku with comali.


Captain of the ship:



We all are familiar with Cooku with Comali contestants and Comalis but have we noticed the captain of the ship,the director?. Cooku with Comali is directed by Mr.Parthiban. From his interviews we could clearly see that he is a talented and humble person with a down to earth personality. But do you know that apart from Cooku with Comali he also directed Kitchen Super Stars which is also a very popular cooking show. The best part is he got the opportunity to direct the show Kitchen SuperStars just after two years of college.


No script for Cooku with Comali:



This fact might have shocked you but it is true . The director and even the team of Cooku with Comali accepted that there are no scripts or dialogues for the show. Everything will be spontaneous and real. Even chef Venkatesh Bhatt admitted that the team would just explain to him about the challenge and tasks of cooking and he would continue to explain in his own style. So eventually the performance of the artists would be so real and they would give their best for the show


Editing - The success sauce:



The editing of even a short film would be very tedious, then imagine editing a show where you have to capture what is happening in the 10 workstations , what is happening in the pantries, what is happening in the judge table,etc. The portion for two days will be shot for an entire day, then the editing of a single week’s show would take about three to four days. In editing the director along with the editing team have to go through all the footage, should have continuity in mind, should choose suitable funny dialogues and present it as a one and hour show. All this hard work has paved the way for the success of cooku with comali.


Pairing is a surprise:



Pairing contestants with the Comalis would be an interesting part of this show. There is a common misconception that the pairing would be faked to pair up interesting combinations of cooks and comalis but it is not true. The secret sauce of Cooku with Comali is to capture the real emotions on set. So there is no point of faking the pairing. Everything is done so genuinely and they themselves would not know who would be paired with whom. To take the suspense to next level, the team has said that the costumes of comalis would not be revealed even to the judges until the point of pairing.


Owner of CWC:



It is very obvious that cooku with comali is a team effort. Contestants, Judges, Vijay stars, technicians , cameraman,director, assistant directors,editors,etc. But do you know who is the boss or leader of this show, well it is the popular media names, Rovoofa mam and Prathima mam. Ravoofa owns two companies namely STYL and Media Masons. Since most of the limelight is on Comalis and contestants it is necessary for us to give some credits to owners and directors as well.


CWC is not a Vijay TV show:



You all are thinking right now that this is a lie , aren’t you? But let us complete the sentence, Cooku with Comali is not a show created by vijay tv , It is created by a film production company called Media Masons. They have produced a lot of shows like Kitchen Super Star, Samayal Samayal with Vekatesh Bhatt, Ini oru Vidhi seivom, Super singer and of course, Cooku with Comali.


The art of improvisation:



Cooku with comali is done with the art of improvisation. Firstly when they formed the format of a humorous cooking show, they never knew that it would become such a big hit. They constantly improved their challenges, analysed which segments are welcomed by people , what are the strengths and weaknesses of every star and then made the change episode after episode. Hence, season 1 of Cooku with comali was an above average show and season 2 of cooku with comali was an extraordinary hit.


Struggle of CWC artists:



Many people today would not know the depth of struggles when they see the success of people. Nothing can be built overnight, such is the story of our CWC stars. They all have come a long way in their journey, the senior chef was humiliated and got slapped with slippers in his initial days, Pugazh worked in mechanic shops to earn his bread, Pavithra Lakshmi was body shamed and suffered an accident before coming this far, Chef Damu was trolled for his profession, Sunitha faced lot of rejections and the list goes on. So if you are at your lowest point in life,don’t stop continuing your struggles, you will definitely get what you deserve at last.


Cooku with comali’s popularity:



Covid-19 was very tragic all over the world, we lost many of our beloved ones to covid.Many people became heartbroken and depressed. But this show on the other hand was a stress buster and gave some feel good emotions in the post covid days. Cooku with Comali shows are trending even today in many social medias and this craze would never stop. This show also spread across states and was also spoken by people of other languages.
