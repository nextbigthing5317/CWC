Popular TV Shows in Tamil

  • 17 March 2022

A person without Humour is like Baai veetu kalyanam without Biriyani. In Today’s trend, Humour is everything that keeps us motivating and stress-free. Even we couldn’t imagine a life without fun. Most of our happy times are captured with our favorite TV shows. In this blog, I will list the popular TV shows in Tamil in recent times.



1)Cook With Comali




One of the super-duper hit shows of Tamil is Cook With Comali. Vijay TV already hosted many cookery shows but this was a massive hit among the audience. This show hooks up an interesting idea of adding a good dose of comedy to cooking. Comali’s like Pugazh, Bala, Manimegalai, Sunitha, and Shivangi are the pillars of the show. Celebrities who were part of this show are now shining on silver screens with great responses. There is no doubt that this show will keep entertaining us in the upcoming seasons too.



2)Bigg boss




Nowadays, everywhere the Hot topic is about the Bigg boss and its contestants. Colorful house, Energetic task, Favourite celebrities, Different Personalities, Varied emotions, and whatnot, this show has everything to keep us engaging even for the whole day. Kamal Hassan was the biggest backbone of this show, his speech and swag are out of the world. This man steals everyone’s heart even in his 60’s. It is also creating many positive impacts in people's life by analyzing different persons in the house. And it is a life-changing platform for the participants of the house. It provides the needed limelight for them. Overall, it is a complete pack of everything.



3)Survivor




As the name says, Surviving is one of the toughest jobs in this show. The participants are left in a deserted island where all the necessities like fire, shelter, food and water have to be found by the participants themselves. Though this show is produced in more than 25 countries and became a massive hit, here it is not as successful as bigg boss. But many have gone crazy for the task of this show which make the audience to sit in the edge of the seat. This show also shown the other side of action king Arjun as a host. Though it is not popular as bigg boss, it has also gained moderate success among the public.



4)Start Music




Another stress buster show next to CWC is Star Music. No doubt, that this show is nothing without our darling anchor Priyanka Despande. The way she carry the show is just treat for the audience. With her humour sense and timing comedy, she has taken this show to the another level. Dj black’s timely counter music and Singers Ajay krishna and Srisha singing adds more spice to the show. Also our favourite stars take us on a roller coaster of music, fun and lots of entertainment.



5)Super Singer




Another Fun musical show is Super Singer. Both Super singer juniors and Super singer seniors are entertaining with our kalakkal anchors Makapa and Prio. Their chemistry and sink was the major reason for the success of this show apart from Singing. Judges are also good entertainers. Every week it has unique themes of rounds to enter the audience. Overall, this show is like watching a feel good movie.



6)Master Chef Tamil




Master Chef Tamil is completely a serious cookery show involving great chefs of Tamilnadu. Enduring many hurdles the contestants are expected to give a mouth watering unique dishes which is a treat for our eyes. Vijay Sethupathi is yet another reason for people to watch this show. Judges articulate their criticisms very well which mostly relates with our thoughts. It not just entertaining it also inspires people who are just everyday cooks realize their dream to become a master chef.



7)Neeya Naana




Only fews shows last for many years, one among them is “Neeya Naana”. We can never think of any hosts other than Gopinath. His clarity in Speech, knowledge about every current affairs are the highlights of the show. Topic of every debate topic is different and thought provoking to the society. This show is just the reflection of our current society.



8)Kalaka Povathu Yaaru




Kalakka povathu yaaru is a very famous comedy show in Vijay Tv. This show is the starting is the starting point for many sucessful actors like Sivakarthikeyan, Robo Shankar, Ramar and the list continues. The participants would be very tallented and exhibit great humour skills. The show has inspired young talents who get the very deserving platform to exhibit their talent to the world.



9)Super Queen




As the name says, it is show for Queens in the Media. It features twelve cini actresses who will be given series of interesting tasks and challenges. The tasks would be so tricky and entertaining and many times it would bring their real face to the viewers.



10)Solvethellam Unmai




One of the controversial talk shows is “Solvethellam Unmai”. It aims to solve the family issues with moderator as Lakshmy Ramakrishnan. Sudha Chandran and Nirmala Periyasamy also hosted this show, but it is not popular as hosted by Lakshmu ramakrishnan. We can mixed reviews for this show from the audience, but it one of the successful shows on Zee Tamil TV so far. Though it has negative reviews, it is the saviour for many people who are struggling in the society. It solved many issues and disputes in the family.
