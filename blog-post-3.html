Unknown Facts about Tamil Bigg boss

  • 23 March 2022

1)Vastu Shastra House




In our Indian Culture, we build houses based on Vastu Shastra. But a reality show’s set is also build based on that. Yes! It is none other than our Tamil Bigg boss. The set house is constructed based on the Vastu Shastra.



2)Agreement & Bond Policy




Every contestants are asked to sign an agreement before entering the bigg boss house. Until the end of that season, the eliminated contestants are not advised to share some secrets of the bigg boss house. Madumitha who was kicked out of the house because of the kaveri issue was asked not to give any interviews to the TV channels and it is also said that her payment settle is delayed because of that issues



3)Penalty Amount




Contestant who voluntarily walk out from the house are asked to pay the Penalty amount to the Channel for quitting the bigg boss. The Penalty amount is such a huge amount from their payment.



4)Reasons behind the Tasks




Tasks in Bigg boss are decided based on the Contestants activities and their reactions. We might have seen that in many task people who are rivals in the house are asked to play against each other to even more lit that fire between them.



5)Limited Groceries




Another interesting fact is, Groceries supplies are also limited to the contestants use, to indirectly creates an urge to fight among themselves, so as to increase the spiceness of the show. We can clearly see this with the weight differences of the contestants before and after entering the bigg boss house.



6)Dark walls of Bigg boss house




We might think that dark color painted Bigg boss houses are eye catchy to watch. Yes it is true, but yet another reason is to induce the aggressviness in the contestants mind. It is scientifically proved that, seeing dark continuously can induce a sense of aggressiveness in peoples mind. That’s why most of our walls are painted in light or neutral colors
