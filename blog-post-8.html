Best cartoons of 90’s kids

  • 1 March 2022

In today’s world of binge-watching and web series. Most of us would be happy to belong to the decade where we would wait the entire day in school thinking of the favorite cartoons which we would watch after getting home. Imagine it’s 2006, on a rainy morning you don’t have to go to school, your mom cooks your favorite food, you just turn on the TV to watch your favorite cartoons. Life was so good back then. But don’t worry that those days are gone, just be happy that they happened and cherish those beautiful memories. Now fasten your seatbelts and come with us, we are gonna take you to the past to visit some of the best cartoons of 90’s kids.



Power rangers:




Power rangers were definitely an inseparable part of our childhood. Each one of us would have imagined ourselves with any of the power rangers. Some of the famous power rangers among children are SPD, Mystic Force, Dino Thunder, Ninja storm, Time Force. The first power rangers ‘Mighty Morphin was started in 1993. In India, it was telecasted in Jetix, then in Disney XD. Today you can watch power rangers on Netflix and a few more OTT platforms.



Ben 10:




The most attractive part of Ben 10 was its humor. Inside every kid, there is Ben, who is willing to save the people from danger when provided with extraordinary gadgets. Ben 10 is aired on cartoon networks. It is created by comic book artist Duncan Rouleau. Initially, it was started as Ben 10:omnitrix and later expanded to Ben 10:Alien Force, Ben 10:Ultimate Alien, Ben 10:Ultimate.



Tom and Jerry:




Any list of cartoons would be incomplete without Tom and jerry. It is undoubtedly the best cartoon and it could be watched by people of any age. William Hanna and Joseph Barbera created Tom and Jerry in the year 1940. It was aired on Cartoon Networks. Tom and Jerry won 7 Oscars and got nominated 13 times.



Scooby-Doo:




Scooby is so cool because it has a mixture of every element like comedy, horror, friendship, and mysteries. Scooby-Doo was started way back in 1969. It was so popular back in the 90s even in India. Many movies were also made out of scooby Doo.



Mickey Mouse:




Mickey Mouse was created in the year 1928 by Walt Disney. Many people love Mickey Mouse because he is the symbol of our childhood and there won't be a single child which does not recognize Mickey Mouse. Mickey Mouse has also won an oscar. Even today we can watch our favorite Mickey Mouse on the Disney channel.



Dragon tales:




Dragon tales was created in the year 1999 by Ron Rodecker and Jim Coane. It was such a relaxing show to watch for children before starting their day. It involves the adventures of two siblings, Emmy and Max along with the dragons. It also taught children much informative stuff in a funny way.



Looney Tunes:




Looney Tunes is a Warners Bros series that started in the year 1930. It has many adorable cartoon characters like Sylvester, Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck,etc. Looney tunes is one of the funniest cartoons ever made. There are many video games that are developed after Looney tunes. Looney tunes can be viewed today on NetFlix.



Spongebob Square pants:




Stepehen Hillenburg created SpongeBob Square Pants in the year 1999.It involves the adventures of sea sponge bob which could talk and lives in a pineapple under the water.The friendship expressed between Spongebob and Patrick star in the show would be so beautiful to watch.The show was phenomenal back then and also received many awards.



Dragon ball Z:




Dragon Ball Z was created by Japanese manga artist Akira Toriyama in the year 1989.It involves the defending of the earth by Goku and the Z warriors from the super-powered fighters and the monsters.Dragon Ball Z is not just a cartoon show, it had perfect characterization and character development of its characters.Apart from the story and actions, the visual transformations used in the cartoon were also epic.



Pokemon:




Pokemon was initially developed as a Nintendo game and later developed as a cartoon.Pokemon was created by Satoshi Tajiri in the year 1996. After that, both the show and the cartoon were a great hit. Later in 2016, the most popular game Pokemon go was created. The monsters in Pokemon cartoon are also so famous, especially Pikachu.
