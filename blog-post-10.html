TV personality turned Actor/Actress

  • 12 March 2022

Hard work can lift people to better heights. According to the saying, people who made their consistent effort in Television got recognized and turned into successful actors and actresses in Tamil Cinema. In this blog, we are going to see many such famous TV personalities turned Actor and Actress of Tamil Cinema.



Siva Karthikeyan:




The Multifacet Actor Sivakarthikeyan is a well-known TV personality who was part of many shows on Vijay Television. He started his career as a stand-up comedian in "Kalakka Povathu Yaaru" and Hosted several trending shows like "Athu ithu ethu", "Jodi", and so on. His Jovial personality attracted many directors of Tamil Cinema. "Marina" becomes his first debutant film, then he played a side role in "3" and did a few flims but he grabbed everyone’s attention with his super hit movie “Varutha padatha Valipar Sangam”. His comedy, Acting, Dancing skills took him to next level as One of the best and most famous actors of the Tamil Flim Industry. He has also started his own production company. Many movies are signed and waiting to release in the upcoming days.



Priya Bhavani Shankar:




Priya Bhavani Shankar is a charming actress in Tamil Flim Industry. But before entering into Cine field, she has worked in News Channel "Puthiya Thalaimurai" as a TV news presenter and also worked as a serial actress in Star Vijay Television's Kalyanam Mudhal Kadhal Varai. Her first film was Meyaadha Maan which was released in the year 2017. Then she acted with many top stars like Karthi, S.J Surya, Harish Kalyan, and the list goes on. She has more than 6 flims lined up for this year.



Robo Shankar:




Robo Shankar is a famous Standup comedian who has appeared in many famous Tamil flims as Comedian and Actor. His early life was no bed of roses. He first made his breakthrough with Start Vijay's "Kalakkapovathu Yaaru" as a Standup comedian. Entered film industry as a side artist in the movie "Rowthiram", but unfortunately his scenes were edited out from the flim. Made his strong debut in the flim "Idharkuthane Aasaipattai Balakumara" in the year 2013, he got appreciation from many critics for his acting and body language. Followed by this flim, he got many movie opportunities and nailed his roles in all his flims and now became one of the favorite comedians and actors. Though he becomes a flim person, he is also working on Television channels as a Judge in many shows and stealing people's hearts.



Vani Bhojan:




Vani Bhojan was a sensational TV actress who is shining in the Cine field with her natural acting skills and stunning looks. Vani Bhojan initially worked as Air Hostess and her appearance in a TV ad lead her to the acting field. She gained her popularity in the Sun TV serial "Deivamagal", her character "Sathya" was recognized famously by everyone and got her many awards and recognition. This success helped her to step into Telugu and Tamil Cinema. First, she appeared in Telugu flim Prema also at the same time she appeared as a judge in TV shows. Later her movies in Telugu became a great commercial success. Finally, she got an opportunity in Tamil flim "Oh my Kadavule" which was a huge success at the box office. Also, started to act in other Tamil Web series and movies. Now she is acting in more than 8 flims in both Telugu and Tamil flim industries.



Santhanam:




Many noteworthy stars have emerged from Vijay TV and one such gem is our Santhanam. He was a lead character in the very famous comedy show Lollu Sabha. Then he first got a chance to act as a comedian in Simbu's manmadhan movie from there on there was no stopping for him. He acted alongside all big heroes and earned an irreplaceable place in tamil cinema.There is no one to beat santhanam in timely comedies, after he went on to become hero there was a void in tamil cinema for comedy. He first starred as hero in vallavanukku pullum aayudham movie. From then on he also earned a respectable name as an entertainer and hero.
