Top 10 Tamil Shows of 90's Kids

  • 25 March 2022

Many shows may come and go, but only a few shows still remain in our hearts. Such shows are done majority in 90's period. Those are the golden period of Tamil Television, where shows are merely created for people's entertainment without indulging in the race of content generation of today's media. In this blog, I will be taking you all to 90's kid famous shows that we watched and enjoyed during our childhood.



1) Top 10 Movies




Top 10 Movies is one of the favorite shows for 90's kids which was aired on Sun TV. It will be telecasted once a week on weekend days. It gives the list of top ten Tamil movies that are released that week. It also includes the clippings of movie scenes along with the audience reactions and interviews. This show was hosted by Dr. R. Suresh Kumar, who is the pillar of this show. His punch dialogues, clear speech, and good pronunciation are all plus of this show.



2) My dear Bootham




My dear Bootham is a fantasy and adventurous based serial in Tamil. It was successfully aired on Sun TV between 2004 and 2007 at 6:30 pm on weekdays. It covers around 904 episodes. A Ghost helping small kids is the one-liner of this serial. The cast of this serial includes Vaiyapuri, Ganthimathi, Nivetha Thomas, Abhilash, Bharath, and many more. Among all the Sun TV serials, it topped the TRP every week. Still no other serial could beat this serial’s concept and success among the children.



3) Lollu Sabha




Lollu Sabha is a comedy series aired on Vijay TV between 2003 and 2008. It paved the way for comedy stars like Santhanam, Swaminathan, Jangiri Madhumitha, Manohar, Yogi babu, and many more. Each episode was a spoof of Tamil flims or Television shows. Santhanam’s timely punch and Manohar’s body language are the best part of this series. One of the best comedy shows for 90’s kids.



4) Thanga Vettai




Thanga Vettai was a Popular quiz-based game show aired on Sun Tv. It was hosted by the versatile actress Ramya Krishnan. Every episode, people will be curious to see Ramya Krishnan's Jewels and Costumes and it has a separate fan base. During its release, it got huge responses from the audience. With its huge success, it was also hosted in Malayalam and Telugu.



5) Shaktimaan




90's kids' first Superhero serial was "Shaktimaan" which was aired on the Doordarshan channel between 1997 and 2005. Kids have gone crazy for this serial. Its popularity is nationwide and dubbed in many Indian languages. It was also used in Business tactics to sell the products, Children loved to buy Shaktimaan labeled items in stores. During that time, every house's Fridge, Washing machine, and Bureau will be labeled with shaktimaan’s stickers. It was such an impactful serial in the Indian Television industry.



6) Pepsi Ungal choice




Pepsi Ungal choice is a celebrity gossip show aired on Sun TV. It entertains the audience and helps them to discover more about the celebrities they admire and love. This show was hosted by sensational anchor Uma, later she was recognized as "Pepsi uma". Uma captivated the audience with her beauty and unique voice. Standing on-screen with Slik saree, half-stretched hair, with a smile all over her face, there was no one who did not enjoy her talking. This was the only show which lasted for more than 15 years in Tamil Television Industry at that time.



7) Paatuku Paatu




Lalithavin Paattukku Paattu is a Tamil Singing reality show that aired on Sun TV. It is a Singing Competition show hosted by Mr. B.H. Abdul Hameed. This show aired on Sundays at 8:30 PM. After the success of the show, it was continued in Kalaignar TV in late 2000. It gained popularity through its unique concept and the voice slang of the host.



8) Thirai vimarsanam




Thirai Vimarsanam is a Movie review show which was aired on Sun TV. It was hosted by Ratna who is a famous Tamil TV anchor. It helped the audience to make a decision about the newly released movies. Clips of the movies will be shown in between the review which creates an air of suspense among the viewers. Along with the movie review, the anchor also comments on the performances of the movie stars, music support, directors' work, and many more aspects of the movie.



9) Koffee with Suchi




Koffee with Suchi was a Celebrity Chat show aired on Vijay TV every week Saturday at 9:00 PM. As the name of the show says, it is a talk show hosted by famous singer and RJ Suchitra. It has various segments like Rapid-fire and Nostalgic moments. This show was very entertaining and engaging. The audience was made to know their stars better with this show.



10) Arattai arangam




Arrattai Arangam is one of the popular Debate show aired on Sun TV since 1996. Anchoring of this show was wonderfully fone by Shri. Visu, Eminent Director, producer, and writer of Tamil Cinema. This show made a record by telecasting for 20 years with more than 600 episodes. It focused on a common issue. The issue will be discussed to find an expecting solution. Later, the show was hosted by T Rajender.
